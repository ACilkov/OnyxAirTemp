Empty Legs

Jet For Less

One of the best-kept secrets of private aviation is the concept of an empty leg flight. These deeply discounted flights are available when an aircraft needs to fly from its base (or current location) to another airport where it has a scheduled departure. Instead of having the airplane fly empty and thereby make no money on the flight, many operators choose to sell these positioning flights at a significant discount.
