Why Onyx Air?

At Onyx Air, we are different and we mean it.

Let’s be honest, there are more air charter brokers than you can count. Zero regulation means that pretty much anyone can enter this business with no vetting or training whatsoever. On top of that, you may find that your broker is nowhere to be reached once you book a flight.

So, what makes Onyx Air different? It's simple.

With You Every Step of the Way

Firstly, we are with you every step of the way. From the moment you request a quote to the moment you disembark the airplane at your destination, our dedicated client service concierge is at your service 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Once you book your flight with Onyx Air, you will have a dedicated flyer experience ambassador who will be available via phone, WhatsApp, text, or email to answer all your questions and to satisfy all your requests. Needless to say, everyone on our team is thoroughly vetted and trained in the highest standards of professionalism and customer service.

The Onyx Air Experience

Secondly, our access to over 15,000 aircraft and countless relationships worldwide ensure that our clients will always benefit from the newest and safest aircraft available on the market. Our industry expertise means that being an Onyx Air passenger is synonymous with flying in utmost opulence, comfort, and safety. We record all our clients’ experiences with and reviews of different operators, thereby ensuring you always get the best treatment available.

Your Safety is Our Top Priority

Last, but most certainly not least, Onyx Air takes every measure to ensure your safety while on board. Our team goes far beyond government safety regulations when it comes to operator safety requirements, and we never, and we mean never, put our valued passengers aboard airplanes and/or operators with less-than-stellar safety records.
